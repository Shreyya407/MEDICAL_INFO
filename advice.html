Health Advice for Immunocompromised Individuals

  • 🧼 Wash your hands frequently and avoid touching your face.
  • 😷 Wear a mask in crowded or indoor public spaces.
  • 🧪 Stay up to date on all vaccinations recommended by your doctor.
  • 🍎 Eat a well-balanced, nutritious diet to support your immune system.
  • 🏡 Limit exposure to sick individuals and avoid unnecessary travel.
  • 📞 Keep in touch with your healthcare provider and ask questions when unsure.
  • 📚 Stay informed with reliable medical sources — like this site!
